Different Foods of the Philippines

The Philippines have a variety of unique foods all around the world, because of our foods this makes our culture very different having different foods depending on the area.

Photo Name Description Origin
Adobo This is a traditional Filipino dish that is loved by many. This dish is slowly cooked and packed with a variety of flavors. It's a savory dish and great for meat lovers. Batangas
Sinigang This dish is loved by almost every Filipino. It'll kick you into action with it's sour flavor making it addicting. This is also considered as a comfort food by many and is considered a national dish. Visayas and Mindanao
Lumpia It's a dish combined with ground pork, carrots, eggs, and different kinds of ingredients, which are all wrapped by spring roll wrappers, creating a compact dish but with a burst of flavor. Manila
Pancit This dish will get you going with its different kinds of ingredients, making you want to grab more portions than you should. This is also a healthy dish consisting of a lot of vegetables and noodles. Binondo
Sisig This dish is a favorite of many and is usually served with a sizzling plate. It's impact is different kinds of ingredients, making it a flavorful dish, and it also has different types: pork sisig, tofu sisig, and more. Pampanga
